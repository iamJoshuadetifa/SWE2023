What is personal Hygiene?

Personal hygiene refers to the practices that individuals follow to maintain cleanliness and prevent the spread of disease-causing germs.
Personal hygiene practices can help prevent the spread of infectious diseases, promote overall health and well-being, and boost self-confidence
